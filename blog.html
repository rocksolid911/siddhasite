Blog

Blogging is a strong passion of mine that I have developed over the years. It's a good learning habit, relaxing and it is also calms my mind. It allows me to reflect about what I have learned and accomplished. I enjoy mostly writing about concepts that I have learned and my most recent projects.

Writing about concepts, forces me to teach myself what I know. I believe the best way to determine if you really understand a concept is how easy it is for you to explain it to someone. Writing about concepts is just a great way to reinforce your understanding of the concept.

In regard to my projects, I always like to showcase the work that I have done and the insights I was able to gather from my data science project. At the end of the day communicating the findings of a data science project to a technical or non-technical audience is an important skill to have.

I write my blogs on Medium.com and publish them.

Feel free to check out my most recent articles and don't be shy to write a comment.
